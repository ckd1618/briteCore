United States: Software Engineer

BriteCore is looking for creative Software Engineers for our Software Development team. Depending on how we evaluate your skills and preferences, you will be either maintaining our current systems or building new replacement modules for BriteCore, our flagship cloud-based insurance software.

Your job will involve...

  • Developing modern single page applications
  • Implementing backend REST and GraphQL APIs
  • Breaking apart the current BriteCore monolith into smaller services
  • Testing and deploying stable products
  • Collaborating with other Product Development engineers

Our Company

BriteCore is an enterprise management platform for Property Casualty Insurance carriers. When a significant liability or property loss occurs, our customers are there to put lives and businesses back together.

Our team manages complex financial and legal transactions at scale through a modern SaaS platform. BriteCore is responsible for the entire P&C value chain including rating, distribution, policy administration, claims management, and financial reporting, among others. Our customers are extremely stable and loyal organizations that depend on us for the majority of their software needs.

BriteCore is a fast-growing company that has raised significant capital as an emerging leader in our industry. Our staff has doubled over the past 12 months as our customer base has expanded in both scope and scale. Our strong reputation for innovation and total commitment to customer success have produced steady growth over many years.
